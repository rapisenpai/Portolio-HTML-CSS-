Summary

His name is Rafhael Lowix A. Homwad. He was born on September 05, 2001 at Barangay Sangab, Maco, Davao de Oro. He is the eldest brother among the three children of Mr. Rameil B. Homwad and Mrs. Sheila A. Homwad. He took his elementary at Carmen Central Elementary School, and he graduated with academic honors at Carmen National Senior High School. In High School, he joined Marching Band Club and TV Broadcasting club. He was a second placer as Technical Director during his DSPC (Division Schools Press Conference) English TV Broadcasting.

He aspires to be a professional System Analyst and an IT teacher in the future because he wants to fulfill his dream and provide for his family. He is looking forward to achieving his life goals and dreams with the help of Almighty God.
